More about us!

Welcome to The Cooking Castle, where every click opens the gates to a culinary realm of endless possibilities. Whether you're a novice cook or an experienced chef, our website is your fortress of flavorful inspiration. Explore a treasure trove of recipes, sharpen your skills with expert tips, and become part of a food-loving community. The Cooking Castle is your kingdom of taste, waiting to be explored. Join us in this epic culinary adventure today!
Cooking Castle, founded in 1986, started as a humble neighborhood bakery in the heart of a small village. With the passion and effort of all the employees our small bakery became a world renowned cooking giant. We now celebrate the launch of our very own cooking website and our cookbooks. Thank you everyone for making this journey possible.

Billie Ron Ceo of The cooking Castle
